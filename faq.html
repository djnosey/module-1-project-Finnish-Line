How much Coffee to Finns drink?

Finland has the highest per capita consumption of coffee in the world. Total Finnish consumption of coffee in 2019 was 66 million tonnes (1.1 million 60 kg bags). With a population of just 5 million people that means a per capita consumption of 12.2 kg annually.

How to say please in Finnsh?

The Finnish Language has no word for "please". “Please” is often incorrectly translated for tourists as “ole hyvä” – however, this is more of an equivalent of “there you go” or “de nada”. In case you still want to be polite and ask someone to pass the vodka, please, you can use the word for “thank you” instead – kiitos.
